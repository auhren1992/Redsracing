About RedsRacing

A Legacy Built on Speed, Family, and Passion

Our Story

RedsRacing represents more than just a racing team—it embodies a father-son legacy that has been built over years of dedication to paved track racing. What started as a passion project has evolved into a competitive force on the racing circuit, with Team #8 becoming a recognized name in the racing community.

Our journey began when Jon Kirsch, a seasoned racer with years of experience behind the wheel, decided to share his love for racing with his son, Jonny. What makes RedsRacing unique is not just our competitive spirit, but the genuine family atmosphere that drives every aspect of our operation. From the shop to the track, racing is in our blood.

Today, both Jon (#8) and Jonny (#88) compete across multiple racing series, bringing excitement and fierce competition to every track they visit. Our team has grown from a small family operation to include dedicated crew members, supporters, and fans who share our passion for the sport.

The Kirsch Racing Family

Jon Kirsch - #8

Jon Kirsch brings decades of racing experience to Team #8. His deep understanding of race strategy, car setup, and track conditions has made him a formidable competitor. Jon's approach to racing combines old-school grit with modern techniques, making him a mentor not just to his son but to younger drivers throughout the racing community.

Throughout his career, Jon has competed in numerous paved track events, earning respect from fellow competitors for his clean racing style and sportsmanship. His experience spans multiple classes and track configurations on asphalt and concrete surfaces, giving him versatility that few can match.

Jonny Kirsch - #88

Following in his father's passion for racing, Jonny Kirsch is building his own racing legacy through competitive indoor karting. Competing at K1 Speed indoor tracks, Jonny combines youthful energy with the racing wisdom passed down from his father. His aggressive yet calculated racing style has earned him podium finishes and continues to develop with each race.

While Jon tackles the paved tracks, Jonny hones his skills on indoor karting circuits, mastering precision driving and racecraft in the high-speed world of electric kart racing. His natural talent and dedication to the sport showcase the family's racing DNA. The future of RedsRacing is bright with both father and son pursuing their racing passions.

Our Racing Philosophy

At RedsRacing, we believe that racing is about more than just crossing the finish line first. It's about the journey, the challenges overcome, and the relationships built along the way. Our team operates on core values that guide everything we do:

  • Family First: Racing brings our family together and strengthens our bond through shared experiences.
  • Sportsmanship: We compete hard but fair, always respecting our fellow racers and the sport itself.
  • Continuous Improvement: Every race is an opportunity to learn, adapt, and become better drivers and competitors.
  • Fan Engagement: Our supporters are part of our extended racing family, and we value their enthusiasm and loyalty.
  • Safety: We prioritize the safety of our drivers, crew, and fellow competitors above all else.

Behind the Scenes

Our race shop is where the magic happens. Between races, you'll find the entire team working side-by-side, preparing cars, testing new setups, and planning race strategy for the Super Cup asphalt oval series. This hands-on approach ensures that every component is race-ready and optimized for performance on race night.

The RedsRacing operation relies on meticulous preparation and attention to detail. From engine tuning to suspension setup, tire management to weight distribution, every aspect of our race cars is carefully considered and tested. This dedication to preparation is what allows us to compete at the highest level on tracks like Grundy County Speedway.

Meet the Crew

RedsRacing is powered by a tight-knit crew that's part family, part lifelong friends, and all heart. Every member plays a critical role on race night and in the shop between events.

Julie Kirsch

Jon's wife and the backbone of the team. Julie keeps the operation running smoothly, handling everything from logistics to moral support. She's the team's biggest cheerleader and an essential presence at every race, making sure the crew has everything they need while keeping the family spirit alive.

Roger Kirsch

Jon's father and the patriarch of the racing family. Roger brings decades of mechanical knowledge and racing wisdom to the team. From car setup and data analysis to hands-on mechanical work, Adam's technical expertise and dedication make him an indispensable part of the team. Josh — Crew

Jon's neighbor and dedicated crew member, Josh is always ready to lend a hand whether it's in the shop or at the track. Together, this six-person team — two drivers, four crew — proves that you don't need a massive operation to compete. What you need is passion, trust, and people who show up for each other. That's RedsRacing.

Community & Fans

RedsRacing is deeply committed to giving back to the racing community and our local supporters. We understand that paved track racing thrives on community involvement, and we take that responsibility seriously. Throughout the racing season, we participate in charity events, support local businesses, and engage with fans who make this sport possible.

Our fan base has grown steadily over the years, and we're grateful for every person who cheers us on from the grandstands. Whether you're a long-time supporter or new to RedsRacing, we welcome you to join our racing family. Follow our progress throughout the season, interact with us on social media, and come say hello at the track!

We also believe in supporting the next generation of racers. Jon and Jonny regularly share their knowledge with young drivers, offering advice and encouragement to those just starting their racing journey. Keeping the sport alive and thriving for future generations is something we're passionate about.

Looking to the Future

As we look ahead to future racing seasons, RedsRacing continues to evolve and grow. We're constantly exploring new opportunities, whether it's competing in different racing series, upgrading our equipment, or expanding our team. Our goals remain focused on competitive excellence while maintaining the family values that define who we are.

The 2026 season promises to be our most exciting yet, with an aggressive schedule that will test our skills across multiple tracks and conditions. We're preparing our race cars, refining our strategies, and building momentum for what we hope will be a championship-caliber season.

Thank you for being part of the RedsRacing journey. Whether you're a sponsor, a fan, or a fellow racer, your support means everything to us. We invite you to follow along as we chase victories, overcome challenges, and continue building our racing legacy one lap at a time.
