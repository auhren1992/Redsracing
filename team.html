The Father-Son Duo

Two Generations, One Passion

Jon Kirsch

The Veteran of the Track

Fueled by a lifelong passion for speed and precision, Jon has been a formidable presence on the American Super Cups circuit. His journey is one of relentless pursuit—for the perfect lap, the extra tenth, and the top step of the podium. For Jon, racing is about the bond between man and machine, pushing the limits together on the ragged edge.

From late nights in the garage to the adrenaline of the green flag, Jon's career is a testament to dedication and the unfiltered reality of life on the track. Now, he's ready to share that journey with his son, passing the torch to the next generation.
