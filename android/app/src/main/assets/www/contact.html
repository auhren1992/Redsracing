Contact Information

RedsRacing Team #8

Whether you're a fan, potential sponsor, media representative, or fellow racer, we're always happy to connect with people who share our passion for paved track racing.

For the fastest response, please use our online feedback form. We check messages regularly and will get back to you as soon as possible.

What to Expect

  • We typically respond within 24-48 hours
  • During race weekends, responses may take slightly longer
  • All messages are read personally by our team
  • For urgent matters, please mention "URGENT" in your message

Media & Press

For media inquiries, interview requests, or press credentials, please include:

  • • Your name and media organization
  • • Nature of your inquiry
  • • Deadline (if applicable)
  • • Contact information

We're happy to provide high-resolution photos, race statistics, and driver interviews for legitimate media requests.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I visit the race shop?

While we love fan enthusiasm, our race shop is not open for public tours due to safety and insurance reasons. The best place to meet the team and see our cars up close is at the track on race day!

Do you sell team merchandise?

We're working on offering official RedsRacing merchandise in the future. Check back for updates, or contact us if you're interested in sponsoring team gear.

How can I support the team?

The best support is showing up at races and cheering us on! For those interested in financial support, check out our sponsorship page for partnership opportunities at various levels.

Are you looking for crew members?

We occasionally have opportunities for experienced crew members. Send us a message detailing your racing experience, skills, and availability, and we'll keep your information on file.

Can Jon or Jonny provide racing coaching?

Both drivers are passionate about helping the next generation of racers. While formal coaching programs aren't currently offered, they're often willing to share advice with young drivers at the track. Feel free to reach out about specific questions or mentorship opportunities.
